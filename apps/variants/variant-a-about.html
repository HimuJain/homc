About ShopEase

We're on a mission to make exclusive member savings accessible to every online shopper — not just those who know where to look.

Our Story

ShopEase was founded in 2019 by a team of retail technology veterans who believed online shopping could be simpler, smarter, and more rewarding. After years working inside large e-commerce platforms, our founders saw firsthand how the best deals were locked behind paywalls, loyalty programmes, and obscure affiliate networks — invisible to ordinary shoppers.

We built ShopEase to change that. By partnering directly with over 2,000 brands and negotiating member-only pricing at scale, we pass those savings directly to our members without the complexity.
