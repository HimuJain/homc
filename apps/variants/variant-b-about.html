Section 2 — Company Background & History

ShopEase was founded in 2019 by a team of retail technology professionals with backgrounds spanning enterprise e-commerce, consumer fintech, and digital media. The company was incorporated in Delaware with principal operations headquartered in San Francisco, California.

The founders identified a structural gap in the online retail market: exclusive member pricing and supplier-negotiated deals were largely inaccessible to ordinary consumers, confined instead to enterprise procurement channels. The company's original hypothesis — that aggregating and surfacing these deals at consumer scale was technically feasible and commercially viable — was validated in the first 18 months of operation.

Since launch, ShopEase has expanded its partner network significantly and now operates across multiple product categories including apparel, electronics, home goods, health and wellness, and travel.

Section 3 — Mission & Strategic Objectives

ShopEase's stated mission is to democratise access to member-level pricing and to make exclusive retail savings accessible to all consumers regardless of purchasing volume or industry connections. The company operates on the principle that information asymmetry in retail markets disadvantages individual consumers and that technology can meaningfully address this imbalance.

Strategic objectives for FY2024–2025 include expansion of the partner network to 3,500 brands, international expansion into the UK and EU markets, and the development of a B2B product line serving HR departments and employee benefit programmes.
